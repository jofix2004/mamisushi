# Sitemap + Wireframe Noi Dung Landing Page Sushi

## 1. Co so dinh huong

Tai lieu nay duoc viet dua tren:

- Brief Q&A da chot
- File dinh huong thiet ke `sushi-landing-direction.md`
- Nguyen tac trong `taste-skill-main/taste-skill/SKILL.md`

### Cac nguyen tac chot de ap vao wireframe

- Landing page 1 trang, cuon tu nhien, gan nhu khong can menu day du
- Hero khong nen di theo kieu centered generic; uu tien split layout hoac bo cuc lech nhe
- Tong the sach, sang, nhieu khoang tho, khong dong hop qua muc
- Chi 1 accent color ro, o day la do
- Motion o muc vua phai, co mot vai diem wow cho 3D sushi
- Visual phai dan truoc noi dung: nguoi xem thay ngon truoc khi thay "re"
- Combo la diem chuyen doi chinh
- Menu gan nhu full, nhung duoc gom nhom va de kham pha bang scroll / carousel / pagination mem

## 2. Sitemap

### Cau truc trang

1. Hero
2. Brand / Y tuong
3. Menu noi bat + Khuyen mai nhanh
4. Combo section
5. Full menu explorer
6. Dat ban + Chon chi nhanh
7. Footer toi gian

### Utility layers

- Floating CTA nho: `Dat ban`
- Scroll cue nhe o hero
- Reservation success state
- Menu item hover / preview state
- Combo card active hover state

### Luong chuyen doi chinh

Luong A:

Hero -> Xem combo -> Xem menu -> Dat ban

Luong B:

Hero -> Xem menu noi bat -> Full menu -> Dat ban

Luong C:

Hero -> Brand / y tuong -> Combo -> Dat ban nhanh

### Muc tieu cua sitemap

- Khong lam trang qua dai do nhieu section phu
- Moi section phai day nguoi dung xuong section tiep theo
- Muc tieu cuoi cung luon quy ve `Dat ban truoc`

## 3. Wireframe Tong The

### Nhip cuon mong muon

- Hero: gay an tuong
- Brand: giai thich vi sao quan nay hop voi nhom tre
- Featured: cho ly do de o lai
- Combo: day quyet dinh
- Full menu: cho kham pha sau khi da co hung thu
- Dat ban: chot hanh dong

### He thong CTA

- CTA chinh: `Xem combo`
- CTA phu: `Xem menu`
- CTA chot: `Dat ban ngay`
- CTA bo tro: `Goi nhanh`, `Zalo`, `Messenger`

### He thong noi dung

- Moi section chi nen co 1 thong diep chinh
- Moi block uu tien 1 title + 1 mo ta ngan
- Gia combo hien noi bat
- Gia mon le chi can hien vua phai, hoac `tu ...`

## 4. Wireframe Chi Tiet Tung Section

## Section 1. Hero

### Vai tro

- Tao first impression bang visual sushi 3D that, ngon mat
- Truyen tai ngay tinh cach brand
- Day nguoi dung xuong combo hoac menu

### Desktop wireframe

- Khung 2 cot lech:
  trai la noi dung, phai la cum 3D sushi
- Header toi gian tren cung:
  wordmark tam + nut `Dat ban`
- Khoi noi dung trai:
  eyebrow ngan
  headline 2-3 dong
  mo ta 1 doan rat ngan
  2 CTA
- Khoi phai:
  1 sushi hero chinh
  2-3 phan sushi phu bay nhe quanh asset chinh
  motion float / parallax rat nhe
- Day section:
  scroll cue hoac dong text nho `Xem combo hot`

### Mobile wireframe

- Wordmark va nut dat ban van o tren, nhung compact
- Visual sushi len truoc hoac chen giua headline va CTA
- Headline toi da 3 dong ngan
- 2 CTA xep doc hoac 2 cot tuy chieu ngang

### Noi dung de xuat

- Eyebrow:
  `Sushi modern for every hangout`
- Headline option 1:
  `Ngon mat. Di an vui. Combo hop ly.`
- Headline option 2:
  `Sushi de ru hoi ban di an bat ky luc nao.`
- Mo ta ngan:
  `Khong gian vi giac gon gang, mon len dep, combo de chon cho nhom ban va nhung bua an ngau hung.`
- CTA chinh:
  `Xem combo`
- CTA phu:
  `Xem menu`

### Motion note

- Sushi hero float nhe theo truc Y
- 1-2 thanh phan co hover parallax
- Khong dung qua nhieu hieu ung xoay, tranh cam giac cartoon

## Section 2. Brand / Y tuong

### Vai tro

- Giai thich "quan nay danh cho ai" va "khac gi"
- Dua ra 3-4 thong diep dinh vi that ngan

### Desktop wireframe

- Layout bat doi xung nhe
- Mot cot text trai
- Mot cot phai la cac block thong diep xep so le, co the xen visual sushi detail nho
- Khong dung 3 card bang nhau

### Mobile wireframe

- Xep doc
- Text mo dau
- Sau do la cac block thong diep theo tung dong / tung cum

### Cac block noi dung de xuat

1. `Mon nhin dep nhung khong dat`
   `Tap trung vao trai nghiem ngon mat, de chon, de ru nhau di.`

2. `Gia de tiep can`
   `Combo ro rang, mon le gon dep, hop voi bua an nhom va nhung lan ghe nhanh.`

3. `Nhat hien dai, khong xa cach`
   `Khong qua cau ky, khong qua sang, van giu cam giac sach va chin chu.`

4. `Di an vui hon khi di cung nhau`
   `Thiet ke menu va combo huong den viec goi mon nhanh cho nhom ban.`

### Motion note

- Block text reveal stagger nhe khi cuon den
- 1 visual sushi detail di qua background rat tiet che

## Section 3. Menu Noi Bat / Khuyen Mai Nhanh

### Vai tro

- Cho ly do o lai tren trang
- Hien ngay "mon gi dang hot" va "deal gi dang de chon"

### Desktop wireframe

- Header section lech trai
- Ben phai hoac ben duoi la mot line khuyen mai ngan
- Khu vuc content chia 2 lop:
  lop 1 la featured items strip
  lop 2 la quick promo panel

### Mobile wireframe

- Header -> promo pill -> featured scroll cards
- Co the cho phep swipe ngang o featured items

### Noi dung de xuat

- Section label:
  `Featured now`
- Heading:
  `Mon de goi nhat khi ca nhom chua biet an gi`
- Mo ta:
  `Bat dau tu nhung cuon maki de an, len hinh dep va de chia nhau tren ban.`

### Featured item format

Moi item gom:

- Ten mon
- 1 dong mo ta ngan
- Tag nho: `Best pick`, `De an`, `Hot today`
- Gia co the an hoac bo qua neu chua can

### Vi du content

- `Salmon Cream Maki`
  `Mem, beo nhe, de hop gu nhieu nguoi.`

- `Ebi Crunch Roll`
  `Gion nhe, de chia, len ban dep mat.`

- `Spicy Tuna Maki`
  `Dam vi hon mot chut, hop nhom thich an ro net.`

### Quick promo panel

- Heading:
  `Deal dang chay`
- Bullet nguyen tac:
  `Dong gia mot so mon vao khung gio nhat dinh`
  `Uu dai theo ngay dac biet trong tuan`
  `Combo hot cho nhom 2-4 nguoi`
- CTA:
  `Xem combo hot`

### Motion note

- Featured strip co hover tilt nhe
- Promo panel co so chuyen dong emphasis rat nhe o so gia / badge

## Section 4. Combo Section

### Vai tro

- Bien combo thanh diem neo gia ro rang nhat cua trang

- Gia va nut hanh dong luon nam trong fold tot

### Structure moi combo

## Section 5. Full Menu Explorer

### Vai tro

- Cho nguoi dung xem gan nhu full menu
- Giu trai nghiem dep, co cap bac, khong bi nhu bang gia kho

### Desktop wireframe

- Section header ben trai
- Ben phai co chi dan nho:
  `Keo de xem them`
- Danh sach theo nhom mon, moi nhom la 1 khoi rieng
- Moi nhom co:
  title
  mo ta ngan
  gia tu...
  day item cuon ngang hoac grid pha scroll

### Mobile wireframe

- Moi nhom la 1 section doc
- Item co the swipe ngang trong tung nhom
- Co sticky mini label chi ten nhom khi cuon neu can

### Gom nhom de xuat

1. `Maki signatures`
2. `Roll de chia nhau`
3. `Mon nhe an kem`
4. `Set theo mood`
5. `Do uong / side neu can`

### Format moi nhom

- Heading:
  `Maki signatures`
- Mo ta:
  `Nhung cuon de vao nhat, hop voi nhieu gu va de goi khi di nhom.`
- Gia:
  `Tu ...`

### Format moi item

- Visual 3D / PNG
- Ten mon
- 1 dong mo ta ngan
- Gia nho hoac bo qua tuy cap do uu tien
- CTA an:
  hover hien `Them vao danh sach quan tam` hoac `Hoi mon nay`

### Vi du nhom + content

#### Nhom 1: Maki signatures

- `Salmon Maki`
  `Mem, de an, hop voi lan dau thu.`

- `Avocado Crab Roll`
  `Vi nhe, nhin dep, hop nhom muon goi de chia.`

- `Tuna Sesame Roll`
  `Them chut dam vi, len hinh van rat gon.`

#### Nhom 2: Roll de chia nhau

- `Crunch Roll`
  `Them ket cau gion, de tao cam giac vui mieng hon.`

- `Aburi Roll`
  `Thom nhe, no hon, hop bua an toi.`

#### Nhom 3: Set theo mood

- `Light lunch set`
  `Gon, nhanh, de vao bua trua.`

- `Hangout set`
  `Hop hoi ban muon goi mot lan cho de chon.`

### Motion note

- Scroll gallery mem, khong giat
- Hover tilt nhe cho tung visual
- Section divider nen dung negative space va border mong, tranh card overuse

## Section 6. Dat Ban + Chon Chi Nhanh

### Vai tro

- Chot hanh dong cuoi
- Cho phep dat ban theo nhieu cach ma van gon

### Desktop wireframe

- 2 khoi chinh:
  trai la form dat ban
  phai la chi nhanh noi bat + nhanh actions
- Ben duoi hoac ben canh co danh sach chi nhanh con lai o dang compact

### Mobile wireframe

- Form len truoc
- Sau do la chi nhanh noi bat
- Cuoi cung la 3 CTA bo tro: `Goi`, `Zalo`, `Messenger`

### Form structure

- Ho ten
- So dien thoai
- So nguoi
- Ngay / gio
- Chon chi nhanh
- Ghi chu ngan
- CTA:
  `Dat ban ngay`

### Chi nhanh block

- Tieu de:
  `Chi nhanh noi bat`
- Noi dung:
  ten chi nhanh
  dia chi ngan
  gio mo cua
  status nho: `De ghe`, `Trung tam`, `De hop nhom`
- Nut:
  `Chi duong`

### CTA bo tro

- `Goi nhanh`
- `Nhan Zalo`
- `Nhan Messenger`

### Microcopy de xuat

- Heading:
  `Dat ban truoc de len ban nhanh hon`
- Mo ta:
  `Chon chi nhanh phu hop, giu cho som va de ca nhom toi la co ban dep de ngoi.`

### Interaction states can co

- Success:
  `Ban da gui thong tin dat ban. Quan se lien he xac nhan som.`
- Error:
  `Thong tin chua day du. Vui long kiem tra lai so dien thoai va thoi gian.`
- Loading:
  Nut `Dang gui...`
