# Brief Landing Page Sushi

## Thong tin tong quan

- Ngay tao brief: 2026-03-18
- Phan khuc: Re, trung binh
- Doi tuong chinh: Gioi tre

## Muc tieu uu tien

- Keo khach den cua hang
- Day khuyen mai / combo
- Hien thi menu ro rang, de chon

## Ghi chu

- Hinh thuc lam viec: hoi tung cau, cap nhat brief sau moi cau tra loi.

## Cau hoi da chot

### Cau 1

Landing page nay uu tien muc tieu nao nhat?

Tra loi:
- Keo khach den cua hang
- Chay khuyen mai / combo
- Menu

### Cau 2

Tra loi:
- Tre trung, vui, nang dong
- Nhat hien dai, gon gang

### Cau 6
