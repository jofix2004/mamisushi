# Copywriting Chi Tiet Landing Page Sushi

## 1. Ghi chu su dung

Tai lieu nay viet de dung truc tiep cho mockup, UI design, va code.

### Quy uoc token

- `[TEN_BRAND]`: ten brand tam thoi hoac ten chinh thuc
- `[KHUNG_GIO]`: khung gio uu dai
- `[NGAY_DAC_BIET]`: ngay ap dung uu dai
- `[GIA_COMBO]`: gia combo that
- `[TU_GIA]`: gia bat dau cua nhom mon
- `[CHI_NHANH]`: ten chi nhanh
- `[DIA_CHI]`: dia chi that
- `[GIO_MO_CUA]`: khung gio hoat dong

### Giong van can giu

- Ngan gon
- Sach
- Hien dai
- Tre nhung khong lo
- Goi mon de hinh dung, de ru nhau di an

### Cach dung

- Neu chua co ten brand, co the tam thay `[TEN_BRAND]` bang `Nami Sushi`
- Neu chua co gia that, giu nguyen token
- Uu tien dung ban copy chinh truoc, ban du phong dung khi can doi layout
