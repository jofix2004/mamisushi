# Dinh Huong Thiet Ke Landing Page Sushi

## 1. Muc tieu du an

Landing page duoc xay dung de phuc vu 3 muc tieu chinh:

- Keo khach den cua hang
- Day khuyen mai va combo
- Trinh bay menu ro rang, hap dan, de kham pha

Trang khong di theo huong sushi cao cap hay xa cach. Dinh vi can ro rang: de tiep can, vui khi di nhom, mon nhin dep, gia hop ly.

## 2. Dinh vi thuong hieu

### Cam giac tong the

- Tre trung, vui, nang dong
- Nhat hien dai, gon gang
- Khong qua sang trong
- Khong qua hoat hinh

### Gia tri cam nhan

- Mon nhin dep nhung khong dat
- Gia de tiep can
- Hop voi di nhom, tu tap
- Combo la diem vao de ra quyet dinh

### Tuyen ngon hinh anh thuong hieu

Sushi ngon mat, hien dai, de ru ban be di an, nhung van giu cam giac sach, gon va khong bi "re tien" trong cach trinh bay.

## 3. Chan dung khach hang

Khach hang muc tieu khong bi bo hep vao mot nhom duy nhat, nhung tap trung vao nhom tre:

- Hoc sinh, sinh vien, nhan vien tre, nhom ban, cap doi tre deu co the tiep can
- Can tranh cam giac cao cap va kho buoc vao
- Uu tien tam ly "di an vui, chup dep, goi combo hop ly"

## 4. Huong noi dung

### Tone of voice

- Ngan gon
- Sach
- Hien dai
- Tre nhung tinh gon, khong lo

### Cach viet noi dung

- Tranh caption qua dai
- Tranh kieu copy qua "quang cao" hoac qua sanh dieu
- Moi block nen co 1 headline ro + 1 mo ta ngan
- Rieng menu va combo co the co them thong tin de giai thich de chon mon

### Thong diep chinh

- Ngon mat, di an vui, combo hop ly

Co the phat trien thanh cac huong headline nhu:

- Sushi ngon mat, combo de ru hoi ban
- Di an vui hon voi combo hop ly
- Mon dep, vi de an, gia de ru nhau di

## 5. Dinh huong visual

### Mood tong the

- Toi gian kieu Nhat hien dai
- Tong sang sach
- It mau
- Diem nhan do ro, tiet che
- Typography thanh manh, sach, hien dai

### Dieu can tranh

- Khong lam theo huong qua sang trong, den muc xa cach
- Khong lam style qua truyen thong Nhat
- Khong dung visual qua cartoon hoac qua "do choi"
- Khong bien landing page thanh mot menu chay chu don thuan

### Hinh anh

- Uu tien model 3D sushi that, ngon mat, gan anh that
- Han che dung anh chup san pham
- Neu can anh that, chi nen dung anh da xoa nen, dong nhat bo cuc
- 3D can xuyen suot toan trang nhung chi la diem nhan nhe, khong pho dien qua muc

## 6. Dinh huong 3D va motion

### Vai tro cua 3D

3D la chat lieu hinh anh chinh de tao su khac biet cho landing page. Tuy nhien, 3D khong duoc lam trang trong hon muc can thiet. Muc tieu la tao cam giac ngon mat, hien dai, sach, de nho.

### Cach ap dung 3D

- Hero: mot hoac vai phan sushi noi bat de tao first impression
- Menu noi bat: 3D ho tro mo ta mon
- Combo: 3D dung de lam cac "set" trong dep mat va de hinh dung
- Full menu: cuon hoac chia nhom, moi nhom co visual rieng

### Motion

- Mac dinh la vua phai
- Co the co parallax nhe, hover nhe cho phan sushi
- Mot vai diem nhan co motion noi bat hon de tao wow
- Luon uu tien su muot, sach, khong roi, khong lam giam kha nang doc noi dung

## 7. Dinh huong bo cuc landing page

Thu tu trai nghiem da chot:

1. Hero
2. Brand / y tuong
3. Menu noi bat / Combo khuyen mai
4. Combo
5. Full menu
6. Dat ban

### A. Hero

Muc tieu:

- Tao an tuong bang visual sushi 3D that bat mat
- Truyen tai ngay tinh cach brand
- Dan nguoi dung di xuong khu vuc combo / menu

Nen co:

- Ten brand tam thoi hoac logo tam
- Mot thong diep ngan
- Visual sushi 3D lam trung tam
- CTA uu tien: xem khuyen mai / combo

### B. Brand / y tuong

Muc tieu:

- Giai thich ngan gon quan nay khac gi
- Nhac lai tinh cach: tre, gon, hien dai, de tiep can

Nen co:

- 2-4 y chinh ve dinh vi
- Layout sach, tho, co khoang trang

### C. Menu noi bat / Combo khuyen mai

Muc tieu:

- Day ngay phan chuyen doi manh nhat
- Cho nguoi dung thay do "hoi ban co ly do de di"

Nen co:

- Dong gia mot so mon
- Giam gia theo khung gio / ngay dac biet
- Combo hot de nhom 2-4 nguoi de ra quyet dinh nhanh
- Gia combo hien rat ro

### D. Combo

Muc tieu:

- Lam ro cac lua chon theo nhom
- Tao cam giac di an chung se vui va tiet kiem hon

Nen co:

- Card combo ro rang
- Visual 3D hoac PNG cua tung set
- Gia noi bat
- Mo ta ngan va de hieu

### E. Full Menu

Muc tieu:

- Cho nguoi dung kham pha gan nhu full menu
- Van giu trai nghiem dep, co phan cap, khong thanh danh sach kho doc

Nguyen tac:

- Gom nhom theo loai mon
- Dung cuon / carousel / khu vuc scroll de trinh bay visual 3D hoac PNG
- Khong trai deu tat ca mon tren mot mat phang
- Mon le chi can hien gia vua phai, hoac gia "tu..." theo nhom

### F. Dat ban

Muc tieu:

- Chot chuyen doi
- Cho phep nguoi dung dat ban theo cach phu hop nhat

Can co:

- Form dat ban tren landing page
- Nut goi dien nhanh
- Zalo / Messenger
- Neu co nhieu chi nhanh, cho phep chon chi nhanh noi bat truoc

## 8. Dinh huong dieu huong va UX

- Uu tien cuon tu nhien, khong can menu day du
- Co the dung cue nho de goi y cuon xuong
- Mobile va desktop can bang nhau, khong thiet ke theo mot nen tang duy nhat
- CTA can xuat hien dung luc, khong lap lai qua nhieu

## 9. Dinh huong gia va trinh bay thong tin

### Gia

- Khong nhan manh gia o moi mon le
- Uu tien hinh anh va su them an truoc
- Combo hien gia noi bat vi day la diem neo quyet dinh
- Menu theo nhom co the dung dang "tu ..."

### Do day thong tin

- Moi section nen gon
- Moi block nen co 1 title + 1 mo ta ngan
- Cho phep section menu/combo giai thich ky hon neu can

## 10. Dinh huong chi nhanh va chuyen doi

- Co nhieu chi nhanh nhung nen co 1 chi nhanh noi bat
- Muc tieu cuoi la dat ban truoc
- Hanh trinh chuyen doi ly tuong:

  Nhin thay hero dep -> bi hut vao combo -> xem menu chi tiet -> dat ban

## 11. Goi y ten tam cho concept

Do ten that chua chot, co the dung ten tam theo huong romaji, de doc voi nguoi Viet, goi Nhat nhung khong qua co dien. Vi du:

- Nami Sushi
- Sora Maki
- Yori Sushi
- Mogu Sushi
- Aki Roll

Day chi la ten tam de thu heading, logo wordmark va tone visual, chua phai ten cuoi.

## 12. Nguyen tac thiet ke cot loi

- Lam nguoi xem thay muon an truoc khi thay muon doc
- Combo la diem chuyen doi ro nhat
- Visual 3D la vu khi khac biet, nhung phai tiet che
- Trang phai tre, sach, hien dai, nhung van de tiep can
- Moi thu nen huong den viec ru mot nhom ban di an vui, de chon, de dat ban

## 13. Hang muc con mo

Nhung muc nay chua can chan ngay, nhung se can o vong tiep theo:

- Ten brand chinh thuc
- Logo / wordmark
- Dia chi chi nhanh cu the
- Thong tin dat ban that
- Danh sach menu that va gia that
- He thong khuyen mai theo ngay / khung gio cu the
